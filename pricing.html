Operational FAQ

What does guided setup include?

Guided setup involves four weekly consultation sessions with our implementation engineers, where we map your existing CSV/Excel workflows into Orchestrate logic and train your team leads.

Who manages infrastructure in Pro?

In the Pro model, you maintain ownership of the server environment (AWS/Azure/GCP), while we handle the containerized deployment of the application via our CI/CD pipelines.

Can we upgrade from Pro to Enterprise later?

Absolutely. Migration from self-managed or guided setups to our fully managed model is seamless and typically involves a 48-hour data transition period with zero downtime.

Need a pricing model aligned to your team structure?

Contact our enterprise architecture team for a custom assessment of your operational needs and infrastructure requirements.
