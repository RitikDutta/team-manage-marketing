# Team Manage

**Team Manage** is a modern workflow and task management platform built to help organizations manage day-to-day work between managers and employees with more clarity, accountability, and structure.

It brings together **task assignment, progress tracking, team communication, calendar visibility, holiday requests, and login activity monitoring** into one streamlined system.

---

## What is Team Manage?

Team Manage is designed for teams that want a more organized way to assign work, track progress, and stay aligned.

Instead of managing tasks across scattered chats, spreadsheets, and manual follow-ups, Team Manage gives managers and employees a shared workspace where work is visible, trackable, and easier to manage.

It is especially useful for organizations that want:

- clear task ownership
- better manager-to-employee coordination
- visual scheduling and calendar-based planning
- simple internal communication
- improved visibility into attendance and requests

---

## Why Team Manage?

Managing a team is not just about assigning work. It is about making sure everyone knows:

- what needs to be done
- who is responsible
- what is in progress
- what is completed
- what is delayed
- who is available

Team Manage helps solve these day-to-day operational problems with a structured system that is practical, easy to understand, and built for real organizational workflows.

---

## Core Highlights

### Task Assignment That Stays Clear
Managers can assign tasks to employees with descriptions, task types, and optional start/end dates and times. This makes planning more structured and reduces confusion.

### Progress Tracking
Employees can update task status as work moves forward, while managers can review and verify completed work.

### Calendar-Based Visibility
Tasks are displayed visually through calendar views, making it easier to understand workload, deadlines, and schedules at a glance.

### Team Communication
Managers and employees can communicate directly inside the platform, reducing the need to switch between multiple tools.

### Holiday Request Flow
Employees can submit leave requests, and managers can approve or deny them through the system.

### Login Activity Monitoring
The platform can also track employee login records, giving organizations more visibility into team activity.

## How It Works

1. Managers create and assign work
2. Employees view and update assigned tasks
3. Progress becomes visible across dashboards and calendars
4. Teams communicate directly within the platform
5. Managers review completion, leave requests, and activity logs
6. The organization gets a more structured workflow overall
