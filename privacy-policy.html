Privacy Program

Privacy Policy for the essential account and workspace data handled by Team Manage.

Team Manage is designed to collect only the information needed to create accounts, operate customer workspaces, process billing, secure access, and respond to support requests. This page explains that limited collection model in clear business terms.

Last updated: March 23, 2026
Focused on essential service data only
Controller vs processor

Team Manage generally acts as a processor for customer workspace content and as a controller for account, billing, and support records needed to run the service.

Minimal collection

We limit collection to business contact details, customer-submitted workspace content, billing records, support messages, and essential security logs.

Limited sharing

We share data only when needed to host the service, process payments, provide support, or meet legal obligations.

1. Information we collect

Team Manage collects only the information reasonably required to provide the service. In most cases, that is limited to business account details, customer-submitted workspace content, billing records, support communications, and essential security or access logs.

Account data

Names, business email addresses, roles, organization names, and the credentials or sign-in records needed to manage user access.

Workspace data

Tasks, approvals, comments, files, and workflow settings that customers or their users choose to place in the platform.

Essential service data

Basic billing records, support requests, and limited security logs used to keep the service reliable and investigate access or support issues.

2. How we use information

Team Manage uses collected information to create and manage accounts, authenticate users, render customer workspaces, process billing, respond to support requests, and maintain the security and reliability of the service.

We do not describe an advertising or data-broker model for Team Manage. Information is handled to operate the product relationship, not to build unrelated marketing profiles.

3. Processing roles and customer instructions

For customer workspace content, Team Manage typically acts on behalf of the customer and processes data in accordance with customer instructions reflected in the product configuration, service agreement, or support requests. Customers are responsible for determining whether they have an appropriate legal basis to collect and use the data they place in the platform.

For account creation, billing, service administration, and support communications, Team Manage generally acts as an independent controller because that information is necessary to operate and safeguard the service relationship.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We may share information with a limited set of service providers that help with hosting, authentication, payment processing, customer support, or security operations. Those providers are expected to handle data under confidentiality and security obligations appropriate to their role.

We may also disclose information where required by law, regulation, court order, or other valid legal process, or where disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the safety, rights, systems, customers, or business operations of Team Manage or others.

We do not sell customer personal information, and we do not share workspace data for advertising purposes. If Team Manage is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset transfer, information may be disclosed as part of that transaction subject to customary confidentiality protections.

5. Retention and deletion

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, meet legal and accounting obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain legitimate business records. Retention periods vary by data type, subscription tier, support history, and applicable law.

Customer workspace data is generally retained for the duration of the subscription and any agreed transition or export period. After termination, data may be deleted or anonymized in accordance with the customer’s contract, internal retention controls, and operational backup cycles.

6. Security and international transfers

Team Manage uses administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Those safeguards include role-based access controls, logging, monitoring, encryption in transit, and operational review practices consistent with the services offered.

Where data must be processed across jurisdictions, Team Manage uses appropriate contractual and operational measures designed to support lawful transfer and consistent handling. No transmission or storage environment can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and customers should configure access settings appropriately for their risk profile.

7. Essential cookies and privacy choices

Team Manage uses only essential cookies or similar storage needed to sign users in, maintain secure sessions, and keep the service functioning reliably. We do not describe advertising trackers within the product experience.

Subject to applicable law, individuals may request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of certain personal information, or object to certain processing. Where Team Manage acts as a processor, requests relating to workspace content should usually be directed to the relevant customer administrator first.

Team Manage may require reasonable verification before fulfilling privacy requests and may retain limited information where necessary to comply with law, security obligations, or legitimate dispute resolution requirements.

8. Policy updates and contact

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product evolution, legal requirements, or operational changes. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Where required, we will provide additional notice through the service or through account contacts.

Questions about privacy practices, data processing terms, or privacy rights requests can be directed through your Team Manage account representative or privacy contact channel identified in your commercial documentation.
